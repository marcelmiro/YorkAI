York's Artificial Intelligence Society

We are one of the first societies in the UK to focus on Artificial Intelligence. We strongly believe the role of AI will soon not only revolutionise, but take over large areas of most industries. Many people are not aware of the true potential of this technology and preference employers are giving on people intermediate knowledge of A.I. systems. Our goal is to provide University of York students with all the knowledge and tools necessary and also propel students from the University of York to some of the most sought careers in almost all career paths.
